ABOUT
I am an experienced designer currently based in Montreal, QC and hailing from Mississauga, ON. I see myself as both a designer and an artist, and love taking opportunities to blend these practices in my work. I have trained as a multifaceted designer with an extremely diverse set of skills.

I am a driven and goal oriented individual and I hope I can bring a unique and fresh perspective to your next project. Outside of the studio, I love to hike with my dog, take my camera for long walks, and cook in any of my remaining spare time.
