Inspiration

Herc Agency

Fantastic and dynamic motion design, with bright and bold colour palettes, HERC’s website means business and is in your face. The branding is modular, such that it can function with any colour scheme (most often reflects the colours in the on screen project.

Jakub Szerszen

An extremely complicated mix of animation and motion graphics, Jakub Szerszen’s portfolio is reigned in by an extremely minimal colour palette, with a dramatic black backdrop.

Y78

A contender for the visual design category, Y78’s website makes use of a very controlled scrolling experience, with a plethora of parallax elements, it makes for a very captivating experience. The site makes use of both full bleed images and small frames on white backgrounds, creating a pleasing contrast. The website also has lots of little design details, decorative elements and small messages that act as wonderful surprises.

Pollen London

Pollen’s portfolio is an excellent example of allowing the works to speak for themselves. They alternate between full bleed images, and a selection of several cropped images in a spread, this achieves an excellent level of visual contrast, aiding in maintaining interest for a large body of work.

Parcel Design

While the website is simple and minimal, parcel is a Toronto based company who pride themselves on environmentally minded production, and cover all aspects of contemporary design under one roof, and also house their own print studio.

Parcel Design

Toyfight is extremely focused on “fun”, they offer end to end branding and concept design, but have created an extremely welcoming environment, which evident through their playful and amusing website.
