A QUATRE: BRAND IDENTITY

Fall 2018
Branding, Graphic Design, Marketing Strategy

Over the course of eight months, I had the pleasure of working with Alexandra Ross-Laquerre in developing the brand identity for her digital marketing and events planning company: A Quatre. A Quatre takes a more holistic and honest approach to digital marketing, offering event planning, social media management, content creation, online ads curation, marketing strategy & other related services.

Over a period of three months, I took an extensive iterative approach in developing her brand identity, working through upwards of 15 iterations to reach a final logo. Following this establishment, I developed social media templates, business cards, a brand book, derivative logos & icons as well as a stationary kit.

At the beginning of 2021, I shifted my focus towards developing a website for Alexandra. The goal was to create a one page "snapshot" of A Quatre, while offering a place for Alexandra to create blog posts, and engage directly with prospective clients.
