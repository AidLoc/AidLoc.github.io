MonstrARTity Creative Community

SUMMER 2019 - 2020
Marketing, Design, Creative Direction

MonstrARTity is a Mississauga based, not-for-profit creative community, responsible for organizing several South Asian art's and music festivals throughout the summer season, as well as deploying a series of youth and senior's programs throughout the entire year. For the past two seasons, I have been a member of the team as the lead graphic designer, in charge of executing large scale advertising campaigns under the direction of the PR & marketing lead. These campaigns included posters, flyers, newspaper advertisements, billboards, public transit ads, social media content, merchandise and video graphics. These assets needed to be prepared in rapid succession with a high degree of accuracy, with special consideration for the organization's internal brand guidelines as well as individual advertisers' specifications.

MonstrARTity's image and brand are constantly evolving, but the tone has always been big, bold and over-the-top (in the best way possible).
