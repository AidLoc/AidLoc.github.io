Journalism Student Association, Concordia University

Concordia's JSA is an organization representing all Journalism Undergraduates, covering duties such as governance, student engagement, fundraising and outreach. After many years they were in need of a brand refresh to bring some new life to JSA's image. We went with a poppy colour palette, and a flat text-based logo that pays homage to the classic Chomsky typeface used in newspapers across the globe. The result is a fun but professional brand image that is sure to catch student's eyes.

McGrath Sales & Marketing

McGrath Sales & Marketing is a local start up, offering services in printing and branded merchandise design. The owner wanted a clean and simple logo & business card that communicated a sense of professionalism, while connecting with his Irish heritage.

Rampart Security

Rampart Security is a small property security firm, that aimed to standout from other security companies with a "modern, new-wave approach". This is reflected in the monotone, clean branding.
