MonstrARTity Creative Community
SUMMER 2019 - 2020
Marketing, Design, Creative Direction

MonstrARTity is a Mississauga based not-for-profit, responsible for organizing several South Asian art's and music festivals throughout the summer season, as well as deploying a series of youth and senior's programs throughout the entire year. For the past two seasons, I have been a member of the team as the lead graphic designer, in charge of executing a plethora of assets. These assets included but were not limited to print marketing materials, billboards, public transit ads, digital marketing content, merchandise and video graphics.

MonstrARTity's image and brand are constantly evolving, but the tone has always been big, bold and over-the-top (in the best way possible).
