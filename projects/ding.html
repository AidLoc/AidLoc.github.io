Ding: 5 Typefaces, 100 Iterations

Winter 2018
Typography, Book Design

"Ding!" was a very early project in my design education, which acted as an introduction to iterative design. In this brief project, we had time to research several typefaces of our choosing, and translate what we learned into 100 typographic experiments. These experiments centred on a expressing a single onomatopoeia, I chose the word "ding". While the style varies greatly throughout sets of iterations, I tried to maintain the same bright, poignant tone of this word.
